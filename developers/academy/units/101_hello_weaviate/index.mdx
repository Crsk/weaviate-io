Welcome!

This is the beginning of your journey through the world of vector searches with Weaviate. This unit will provide you with a comprehensive overview of the fundamentals of Weaviate and vector search.

You'll first gain an understanding of what Weaviate is, and what it can do. You will then learn about vector search, which is a method of finding objects by their similarity. This is a crucial concept in Weaviate, and is the foundation of many of the search strategies and tactics that you will encounter in the course.

By the end of this unit, you will have a strong foundation of knowledge that will help you to effectively navigate the rest of the course, and for using Weaviate in your own projects.
