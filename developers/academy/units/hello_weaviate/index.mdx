Welcome to `Hello, Weaviate: An Overview`. 

This unit is the perfect starting point for your learning journey, as it provides an overview of the fundamentals of Weaviate and vector search.

You'll start by gaining an understanding of what Weaviate is and what it is used for. Then, you'll learn about vector search, which is a method of finding data objects that are similar to a given vector. This is an essential concept in Weaviate, as it forms the basis of many of the search strategies and tactics that you will learn about later in the course.

By the end of this unit, you will have a great deal of context for the rest of your journey through the world of vector searches in Weaviate. You'll be well-equipped to continue your learning and build a solid foundation for your future work with Weaviate.
